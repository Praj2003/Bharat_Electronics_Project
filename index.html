About Us

Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) is a leading aerospace and defense electronics company in India. We specialize in the design, development, and manufacture of advanced electronic products for ground and aerospace applications.

With a rich heritage of over six decades, BEL has been catering to the requirements of the Indian Armed Forces and other defense establishments. Our state-of-the-art facilities and skilled workforce enable us to deliver high-quality, reliable, and cutting-edge solutions to meet the evolving needs of our customers.

At BEL, we are committed to innovation, excellence, and customer satisfaction. Our dedicated team of engineers, scientists, and professionals work tirelessly to ensure that our products and services meet the highest standards of quality and reliability.

Our Projects

BEL has successfully executed numerous projects in collaboration with various organizations and government agencies. Some of our notable projects include:
